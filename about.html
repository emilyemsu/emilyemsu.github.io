EMILY SU




Hi!

My name is Emily Su, and I'm currently a junior studying Information Systems & Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm a UX/UI designer by trade, but I love to letter, bake, run, explore, and take photos as well. I've grew up in San Jose, California and have always been surrounded with and fascinated by the intersection of technology and humans, and I'm constantly trying to design solutions to bridge the gap between complex technologies and systems with human understanding.

Random fun fact: I climbed up and down Mt. Fuji for 10 hours straight two summers ago to watch the sunrise on the peak.
