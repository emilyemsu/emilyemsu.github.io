Dishcovery

A hackathon project to help discover your next food adventure.

Background

At the hackathon Hack The North this past October in Waterloo, Canada, my teammates and I were drawn to tackling the Yelp Challenge as one of the sponsor categories: inspire adventure. From there, we created Dishcovery, as we firmly believe that the best way to get to know the city is through its food. From this starting point, we created a web app that builds your own food adventure with a couple of simple fields, including center of the adventure,


I designed the UX/UI of the project, while learning web development tools. What I learned most out of my first hackathon experience is how crucial a functioning application is--while we wanted to make the application as comfortable to the user as possible, we need to get a functioning prototype before stylizing and otherwise.
